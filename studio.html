The Studio

studio bellish

A young, dynamic architecture studio designing residential, office, and commercial buildings — with an emphasis on innovation, sustainability, and minimalism.

The Studio Est. 2020 — Israel

a small practice, with a long attention span.

We believe quality architecture not only enhances quality of life but also contributes to the environment. Our team invests deep thought into every project, using advanced technologies and sustainable materials to create solutions that blend aesthetics with functionality — spaces that encourage positive experiences and invite communities to thrive within them.

How we work
01

listen to the site

Every design begins on the ground — orientation, light, slope, the neighbours, the view you keep and the one you frame.

02

design in dialogue

We work closely with each client through models and drawings, refining the plan until it fits the life it is meant to hold.

03

build with care

Honest materials, resolved details, and a presence on site through construction so the finished house is true to the drawing.
