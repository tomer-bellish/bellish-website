accessibility statement

www.bellish.co.il is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for all users, regardless of ability. We offer a range of accessibility tools and features through our accessibility toolbar to provide an inclusive and seamless experience.

features

Screen reader: assists users with visual impairments by reading site content aloud.

Keyboard navigation: allows users to navigate the site using the keyboard only.

Voice navigation: lets users control the site using voice commands.

Text reader: provides an easy way to have text read aloud.

Contrast options: switch between dark and light contrast to match visual preferences.

Saturation settings: adjust between high and low saturation, or switch to black-and-white mode for visual simplicity.

Content adjustments: change text size, text spacing, line height and letter spacing for improved readability.

Text alignment: align text left, right or center for your convenience.

Link and heading highlighting: highlights links and headings for easy location of key information.

Readable fonts: enable more legible fonts for better clarity.

Large cursor: enlarge the cursor for improved visibility.

Stop animations: pause all animations to reduce distraction.

Reading aid: use a reading mask or reading guide to help focus on specific parts of the text.

Page structure: navigate easily through the structure of the page.

Dictionary: get definitions of difficult words directly on the page.

Hide images: hide images to focus on textual content.

Tooltips: enable information tooltips for additional explanations of elements.

assistive technology support

This website is compatible with a wide range of assistive technologies, including screen readers, voice-recognition software and other devices designed to help users navigate digital content. We are committed to ensuring our website works effectively with these tools.

contact us for accessibility support

If you experience any difficulty accessing content or require assistance, you can report an issue directly through the "Report a problem" section of this website. We are committed to addressing all accessibility issues and continuously improving the accessibility of our website.

disclaimer

While we strive to ensure the website is fully accessible and complies with the most current accessibility standards, some third-party content or external links may not fully meet accessibility guidelines. We continuously work to improve the accessibility of this website and welcome feedback on any issue you may encounter.
